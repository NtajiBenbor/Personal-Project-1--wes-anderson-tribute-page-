WES ANDERSON

BIO

Wesley Wales Anderson is an American filmmaker. His films are known for their eccentricity, unique visual and narrative styles, and frequent use of ensemble casts. They often contain themes of grief, loss of innocence, and dysfunctional families. Anderson is cited by some critics as a modern-day example of an auteur.

Born: 1 May 1969 (age 54 years), Houston, Texas, United States.
Partner: Juman Malouf (2010–)

My Top Three Wes Anderson Movies

The French Dispatch

Duration: 9.0

Cast: tilda swinton, adrian brody, tom hanks

Year: 2023

In "The French Dispatch," the factual weekly report, The French Dispatch, is about to shut down. Editor-in-chief Arthur Howitzer Jr. gathers the best expatriate journalists for the final issue in the charming city of Ennui-sur-Blasé, Paris, France. The movie showcases three feature articles focusing on various intriguing characters, including tormented genius artists, statuesque prison officers, flamboyant critics, moody idealists, conflicted ace reporters, legendary chefs, and the police.

The Grand Budapest Hotel

Duration: 9.0

Cast: tilda swinton, adrian brody, tom hanks

Year: 2023

This movie recounts the adventures of M. Gustave (Ralph Fiennes), a legendary concierge at a famous European hotel between the wars, and Zero Moustafa (Tony Revolori), the lobby boy who becomes his most trusted friend. The story involves the theft and recovery of a priceless Renaissance painting and the battle for an enormous family fortune - all against the backdrop of a suddenly and dramatically changing continent.

Asteroid City

Duration: 9.0

Cast: tilda swinton, adrian brody, tom hanks

Year: 2023

Following a writer on his world famous fictional play about a grieving father who travels with his tech-obsessed family to small rural Asteroid City to compete in a junior stargazing event, only to have his world view disrupted forever.
